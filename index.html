Essential and dynamic, Sibylla BT is a suspension lamp with
a slim, elongated form made of a simple aluminum bar that is
height adjustable. Available in three different lengths: S - 143 cm,
M - 176 cm and L - 209 cm, Sibylla BT creates surprisingly novel
lighting scenarios thanks to its sliding system, allowing the
user to vary the distance from the ceiling and the illuminated
surface. The sophisticated dynamic light LED technology allows
the user to control the intensity and the color temperature of the
light. Using the LCI Light App (download link in the instruction
manual), you can easily switch from 2700 degrees Kelvin, which
corresponds to the light just before sunset, to 6300 degrees
Kelvin, creating the effect of the sun in broad daylight.

dalla forma allungata e sottile, costituita da una semplice barra
in alluminio regolabile in altezza. Disponibile in tre diverse
lunghezze, S - 143 cm, M - 176 cm e L - 209 cm, Sibylla BT crea
scenograﬁe luminose sempre nuove anche grazie al sistema
